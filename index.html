About me
LUKE BOUTIN

I am a young, hungry (literally and metaphorically) and motivated designer from New Hampshire. I am always looking to learn more and be creative. I am currently 22 years old and putting myself through design school. It has always been a dream since childhood to be creating the way I am now. I have always had a passion to uplift and inspire through my work. For me it’s creating or it’s nothing. I love what I do and I am grateful to be able to do it. On top of school, I manage creative media and event planning for Mcintyre Ski Area, as well as freelance design work. I hope to one day run my own business and make a living from my creative talents.
